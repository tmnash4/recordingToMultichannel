Whisper by Treya Nash

with poetry by Thomas Hardy

Whisper is an interactive piece that asks you to record snippets of whispered speech into your phone and upload them to become part of the collective soundscape. You will be given lines of a Thomas Hardy poem to record. Instructions will guide you through the process. Momentarily, a 'start' button will appear. Click the button to continue.

Step 1: Press 'start recording' once to start recording

Step 2: Once you hit record, a line of poetry will appear here. Whisper it into your phone.

Step 3: Press 'stop recording' once to stop recording

Step 4: Upload or delete your audio

Step 5: Repeat!

Please hold the phone further from your mouth :D
